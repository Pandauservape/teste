Tomo: Endless Blue, it's an action RPG in an open world with an anime style.


Welcome to the Endless Blue, an ocean realm inhabited by mysterious creatures known as the Tomo.

Team up with your companions to fight, or simply enjoy daily life. Build vehicles and contraptions to explore procedurally generated islands and villages, and meet unique characters along the way.

Story

When worlds end, fragments of their civilizations become islands adrift in the Endless Blue. At its heart lies the secrets of the Vagari, an ancient civilization long vanished.

Will you unveil the secrets they left behind?
